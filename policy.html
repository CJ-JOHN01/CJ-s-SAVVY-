Refund Policy

Our Commitment

We strive to provide excellent products and services. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent. Please read the following terms carefully.

1. Online Store (Physical & Digital Products)

  • Physical Products: We offer a 14-day return and refund policy for unused physical items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging.
  • Digital Products: Due to the nature of digital products (e-books, templates, courses), we have a "no refund after download/consumption" policy. Once a digital file is downloaded, it cannot be returned. However, we may offer store credit or a satisfaction guarantee in certain cases.

2. Freelance Agency (Services)

For our service-based offerings (AI automation, digital marketing, graphic design), we have a "no refund after work has started" policy. Our payment structure is as follows:

  • A 50% non-refundable upfront deposit is required to secure our services and begin work.
  • The remaining 50% is due upon project completion or at agreed-upon milestones.

3. Blogging (Monetized Content)

For subscriptions, courses, or premium content sold through our blog:

  • We may offer a 7-day trial or refund window to build trust.
  • After the trial period, or once the content has been accessed, all sales are final.

4. Best Practice (Hybrid Approach)

Our tiered refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent:

  • ✅ Refunds for unused physical items (within 14 days).
  • ❌ No refunds for digital downloads once accessed.
  • ❌ No refunds for services already started (deposits are non-refundable).
  • ✅ We provide clear written terms and an FAQ to ensure you are informed before making a purchase.

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at cjssavvycollection@gmail.com.
